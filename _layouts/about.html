09/2025
Our paper "Fairness-Regularized Online Optimization with Switching Costs" is accepted by NeurIPS'25. This paper studies robust online decison-making with long-term fairness consideration and is motivated by many real-world applications such as balancing the public health impact of AI data centers across different regions. Our guest article "The Real Story on AI's Water Use" is published on IEEE Spectrum and clarifies some key facts of AI's water footprint to facilitate evidence-based public discussions.

09/2024
I'm glad to share four recent papers accepted by SIGMETRICS'25 and NeurIPS'24. (1) The SIGMETRICS'25 paper "Learning-Augmented Decentralized Online Convex Optimization in Networks" proposes a novel algorithm to provably robustify machine learning predictions for decentralized optimization in networks. (2) The NeurIPS'24 paper "Bileve: Securing Text Provenance in Large Language Models Against Spoofing with Bi-level Signature" proposes a novel anti-spoofing watermarking method to safeguard LLM-generated content. (3) The NeurIPS'24 paper "Online Budgeted Matching with General Bids" proposes provably-competitive online algorithms for budgeted matching with arbitrary bid sizes. (4) The NeurIPS'24 paper "Safe Exploitative Play in Stochastic Bayesian Games with Untrusted Type Beliefs" quantifies the impact of potentially incorrect type beliefs on an agent's payoff in Bayesian games.
