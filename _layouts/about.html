news

10/2023
I attended the NSF Computer Systems Research PI meeting and was invited to give a talk on my research "Towards Environmentally Sutainable and Equitable Computing" at the panel on Sustainable Computing.
09/2023
I received two new grants as PI from the NSF Design for Environmental Sustainability in Computing (DESC) program: (1) A new user-in-the-loop approach to water-sustainable computing (in collaboration with Mohammad A. Islam from UT Arlington); and (2) A market-driven approach to designing sustainable multi-tenant data centers (with co-PI Daniel Wong).
09/2023
We have three papers accepted by NeurIPS 2023. Our papers study learning-augmented online decision-making and contribute to foundational algorithms to address emerging algorithmic challenges for computational sustainability.
09/2023
I was invited to speak about our research on AI sustainability at the panel "Is generative AI sustainable? Learn about its environmental impact" (with Prof. Sajjad Moazeni from the University of Washington) organized by TechTarget.

societal impacts

Our research has extended well beyond academic publications and generated broader societal impacts in various ways, raising public awareness of AI+sustainability. It has been reported and covered by 800+ media outlets in ~100 countries, including major news outlets such as The Associated Press (also republished in ABC News, Fortune, CNBC, The Hill, etc.), The Wall Street Journal, CBS News, Bloomberg, Fox News, CBC, Financial Times, The Guardian, The Times, The Independent, New Scientist and The Atlantic, among others.
