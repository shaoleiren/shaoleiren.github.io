My research broadly focuses on AI for Good and strives to build a resilient, safe and healthy future. I am interested in AI-augmented decision making that infuses domain knowledge into intelligent agents to optimize decisions with minimum resources and provable performance guarantees, addressing societal challenges such as public health. Additionally, I am interested in making AI safer and more efficient.
